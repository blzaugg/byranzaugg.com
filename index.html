Hello, I’m Byran.
I develop and design for the web.

First, I came from a print design background and was given the opportunity to try my hand at web design—I liked it.

Later, I was given the chance to dabble in web development—I loved it!

Today, I’m a die-hard for beautifully consistent and usable websites; that are models of UI/UX perfection. I enjoy seeing the results of my labors proving invaluable to end users. I have great pride in the solutions I produce. I'll spend the extra effort, to fix that browser quirk, that’s keeping a product from being just right.

I really enjoy taking someone's problem, thinking through the options, and producing a solution. I find it greatly rewarding when I'm able to to improve their situation.

While you’re here, please have a look at my resume and portfolio. I think you’ll love what you see.
